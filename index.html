• Apple Apple Apples are one of the most popular fruits — and for good reason. They're an exceptionally healthy fruit with many research-backed benefits.Apples are also a rich source of polyphenols.They’re likely responsible for many of the health benefits.
  • Grapes Grapes Grapes are grown in temperate climates across the world, including Southern Europe, Africa, Australia and North and South America.Grapes offer a wealth of health benefits due to their high nutrient and antioxidant contents.
  • Strawberry Strawberry Strawberries are bright red, juicy, and sweet.They’re an excellent source of vitamin C and manganese and also contain decent amounts of folate (vitamin B9) and potassium.
